Our Services

We are providing services related to environmental permits, supervision in carbon pricing, and development and planning of Net Zero Emission (NZE) targets for companies.

Our experience includes supporting companies in building ESG frameworks and Net Zero Emission strategies for sectors such as oil and gas, mining, and ports.

  • Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) & permits
  • Carbon pricing strategy and market instruments
  • Net Zero Emission (NZE) roadmaps for companies
  • ESG policy planning and implementation
  • CSR management and environmental communication
  • Technical consulting for sustainable practices in industry
