Our Services

We provide services of environmental permits and supervision in carbon pricing, as well as planning and development of Net Zero Emission (NZE) targets for companies. Our team is experienced in assisting businesses from sectors such as oil and gas, mining, and ports in formulating ESG and NZE strategies.

  • Environmental permits
  • Supervision in carbon pricing
  • Development of Net Zero Emission targets
  • ESG implementation planning
  • CSR program development
  • Support in forestry rehabilitation and conservation projects
